Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Accessible Finance is an independent educational publication. We respect your privacy and collect as little data as possible. This policy explains what we do collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter via Substack.
  • Basic analytics — anonymous page-view counts and referrer information used to understand which guides are most useful. We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

How we use your information

Your email is used solely to deliver the newsletter you signed up for. We never sell, rent, or share your email with third parties. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of every newsletter.

Third-party services

We use Substack to host our newsletter and Google Fonts to serve typography. These services have their own privacy policies. Our referral links to Wealthsimple and Rakuten may set tracking cookies if you click them — those are governed by the destination site's policy.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your email from our newsletter list at any time by unsubscribing or emailing us. We have no other personal information about you to delete.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the contact link in our newsletter.
