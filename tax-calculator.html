Know the limits

A clean estimate, not a tax return.

  • Bracket tax only

    The calculator applies 2026 marginal brackets to the income entered. It does not claim credits such as the basic personal amount or disability, tuition, medical, donation, or age credits.

  • Province matters

    Provincial and territorial brackets apply based on your province or territory of residence at year-end. Quebec also receives a federal abatement, included here as a simple reduction to federal bracket tax.

  • RRSP and TFSA treatment

    RRSP contributions are treated as deductible against taxable income. TFSA contributions are tracked as savings, but they do not reduce current-year income tax.

  • Get advice for edge cases

    Self-employment, business income, capital gains, RRSP deductions, split residency, multiple jurisdictions, and benefits can all change the final result.
