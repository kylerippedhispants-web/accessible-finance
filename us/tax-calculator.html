Read the result correctly

Three numbers that answer different questions

A bracket rate is not the percentage applied to every dollar you earn.

Marginal rate

Your next taxable dollar

The federal bracket applying to the next layer of taxable ordinary income in this simplified estimate.

Effective rate

Your estimated tax divided by income

A broad average that is usually lower than the marginal rate because lower layers use lower rates.

Taxable income

Income after adjustments and deductions

The amount that actually enters the ordinary-income brackets, not necessarily the number at the top of a pay stub.
