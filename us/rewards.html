Explore prediction markets with caution

Polymarket offers event-based markets whose prices can move as participants respond to information and expectations. Outcomes can be uncertain, losses can happen quickly, and participation is not appropriate for everyone.

  • Confirm that the platform and specific market are available and permitted where you live.
  • Read current funding, trading, settlement, withdrawal, fee, and identity-verification terms.
  • Use only money you can afford to lose without affecting bills, emergency savings, or long-term goals.
  • Do not treat a referral or promotional benefit as a reason to take more risk.

Keep the product in the right category

Prediction markets may be interesting, but they should not displace the financial foundation supporting your day-to-day life and future goals.

01

Not an emergency fund

Money needed for rent, food, health costs, debt payments, or near-term surprises should remain stable and accessible.

02

Not a retirement plan

Event contracts do not replace diversified, long-horizon investing inside accounts chosen for your goals.

03

Not guaranteed income

A market price is not a promise. Your position can lose value, and settlement depends on the market's stated rules.
